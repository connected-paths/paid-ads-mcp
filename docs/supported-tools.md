# Supported Tools

Paid Ads MCP supports marketer workflows across Google Ads and LinkedIn Ads.

## Google Ads

Typical capabilities include:

- customer discovery and hierarchy lookup
- campaign discovery
- account and campaign analysis
- search term analysis
- performance reporting
- weekly grouped reporting
- recommendations
- keyword ideas and forecasting

## LinkedIn Ads

Typical capabilities include:

- ad account discovery
- campaign and campaign group discovery
- campaign analysis
- creative comparison
- account, campaign, and creative analytics
- demographic reporting
- weekly grouped reporting
- targeting and audit workflows

## Use cases

- campaign performance reviews
- weekly reporting
- optimization diagnostics
- ad and keyword comparisons
- marketer Q&A in AI clients using live ad-platform context
